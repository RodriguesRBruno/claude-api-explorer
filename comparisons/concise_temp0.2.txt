**Question 4 of 9:**

Given the code below, what does `z` hold after execution?
```python
x = 5
y = x
x = 10
z = y
```

A) 5  
B) 10  
C) 15  
D) Raises a NameError

What's your answer?


💡 Hint: Integers are immutable, so changing `x` doesn't affect `y`.


Incorrect. When `y = x` executes, `y` gets the value 5; reassigning `x = 10` doesn't change `y`, so `z = y` assigns 5 to `z`.
